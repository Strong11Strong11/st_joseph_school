**St. Joseph Mission School Website**

**Overview**
A modern educational website for St. Joseph Mission School that serves as a digital hub for students, parents, staff, and the community. It provides official information about the school while enabling staff-controlled content management.

**Key Features**
1. Public Features (Everyone Can Access)

**Home Page:** School introduction, latest announcements, quick links

**About Section:** School history, mission, vision, values, leadership

**Academics:** Curriculum, programs, teaching methodology

**Admissions:** Process, requirements, important dates

**News & Events:** School activities, achievements, calendar

**Contact:** Location map, contact form, office hours

**Gallery:** Photo albums of events, campus, activities

**2. Staff Login & Admin Features** 
Staff members can log in to manage content:
**Content Management:**
News Management

Create, edit, publish school news

Add images/videos to news posts

Schedule news publication dates

Categorize news (Announcements, Events, Achievements)

**Resource Upload System**

Academic Calendar: Upload PDF/Excel of yearly schedule

Admission Forms: Digital forms for new applicants

Application Forms: Various school application documents

Study Materials: Resources for students/parents

Circulars/Notices: Official school notices

**Document Management:**
Upload files (PDF, DOC, Excel, Images)

Organize by categories (Academics, Admissions, Administration)

Set visibility (Public/Staff-only/Parents-only)

Version control for updated documents

**Admin Dashboard:**
User management (Staff accounts, permissions)

**Content moderation**

Analytics (page views, downloads)

Backup & restore functionality

**3. User Roles & Permissions**
**Super Admin:** Full control over everything

**Staff/Teachers:** Upload resources, post news, manage their content

**Parents/Students (future):** Access specific resources

**Technical Features**
**Frontend (User-facing):**
**Responsive Design:** Works on mobile, tablet, desktop

**Modern UI:** Clean, professional education theme

**Fast Loading:** Optimized images and content

**Search Functionality:** Find news/resources quickly

**Download Center:** Easy access to all documents

**Backend (Staff-facing):**
**Secure Login**: Password-protected staff access

**WYSIWYG Editor:** Easy content creation (like Microsoft Word)

**File Management:** Drag-and-drop uploads

**Preview Mode:** See how content looks before publishing

**Bulk Operations:** Upload multiple files at once
