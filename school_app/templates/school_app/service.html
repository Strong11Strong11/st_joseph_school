Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

We accept students from age 3 (Pre-School) through age 12 (Primary). Our programs are divided into Pre-School (3-5 years), and Primary (6-12 years).

The admission process involves: 1) Submitting an application form, 2) Academic assessment, 3) Parent interview, and 4) Submission of required documents. Please contact our admissions office for detailed information.

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (football, basketball, athletics), arts (music, drama, visual arts), clubs (science, debate), and community service programs. Activities are designed to develop various skills and talents.

Student safety is our top priority. We have 24/7 security personnel, regular safety drills, and a comprehensive child protection policy. All staff undergo thorough background checks and safety training.
